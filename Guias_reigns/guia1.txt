There are also event flags (like having certain buildings or doing certain actions, etc.) that allow for different options. I don't state it here but events have a weighted chance of appearing and may be repeatable (though may be unavailable for a certain amount of time).

Results are in Religion/Population/Army/Money order. Yes/No is really Left/Right.
